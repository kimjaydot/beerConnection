About

Beer Connection was created to help you locate the nearest facilities to your current location that serves beer and provides Wi-Fi services. Simply enter the current zip code you are in and WALAH! You now have options of places to go to have a cold one, network, and program!

Inspiration for the application came from four individuals who wanted to find a nice relaxing place to go later in the day to unwind, code, and enjoy a nice cold brewsky!
